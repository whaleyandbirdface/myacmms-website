  • About

    Arts and Cultural Management and Museum Studies (ACMMS) at Michigan State University is a multidisciplinary program that provides students with the knowledge and skill set to succeed in creative, artistic, and administrative positions within arts and cultural organizations. Students pursue practical opportunities related to the management of, and advocacy for, arts and cultural organizations and entrepreneurial artistic careers.

    We are proud to announce our new partnership with Goldsmiths, University of London! Join ACMMS at Goldsmiths this summer to take classes and explore cultural institutions in London! Open to minors and MA students. More information HERE.
